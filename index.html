Don't have an image URL? Here's how (Imgur)
  1. Go to imgur.com — no account needed.
  2. Click "New post" and upload or drag in your map image.
  3. Once it's uploaded, right-click the image and choose "Copy image address" (on phone: press and hold the image, then "Copy image link").
  4. Paste that link into the Image URL field above — it should end in .jpg, .png or .gif.
